Get Involved {#get-involved .entry-title}
============

<div class="entry-content">

The Australian Government has released a draft of Australia’s first ever
Open Government National Action Plan for public consultation. This is
the final round of consultation in a year-long process to develop the
National Action Plan.

The draft National Action Plan can be accessed / downloaded here:

-   PDF: [Australia’s first Open Government National Action
    Plan](../../files/2016/10/Australias-first-Open-Government-National-Action-Plan-Draft-for-consultation-Accessible.pdf)
-   DOCX: [Australia’s first Open Government National Action
    Plan](../../files/2016/10/Australias-first-Open-Government-National-Action-Plan-Draft-for-consultation-Plain-Text.docx)
-   HTML: [Australia’s first Open Government National Action
    Plan](../../draft-national-action-plan/index.html)

You can find out more about the public consultation process through this
fact sheet:

-   PDF: [Consultation Fact
    Sheet](../../files/2016/10/NAP-fact-sheet-Accessible-extended.pdf)
-   DOCX: [Consultation Fact
    Sheet](../../files/2016/10/NAP-fact-sheet-Plain-Text-extended.docx)

<span style="text-decoration: underline">Make a submission</span>

Public submissions close at **5pm AEDT on Friday 18 November 2016**, and
can be made by emailing <ogp@pmc.gov.au> or by sending a hard copy to:
Public Data Branch, Department of the Prime Minister and Cabinet, PO Box
6500, Canberra, ACT, 2600.

Submissions will be published unless otherwise advised.

Submissions can be accessed/downloaded here: [Public Consultation
page](../public-consultation/index.html)

<span style="text-decoration: underline">Join the conversation</span>

You can also join the open government conversation online using \#OGPAu
or through civil society forums on
[www.opengovernment.org.au](http://www.opengovernment.org.au/)

<span style="text-decoration: underline">OGP Working Groups</span>

We are also encouraging individuals and organisations to connect with
the Open Government Partnership working groups to contribute to peer
exchange and learning across the partnership.

The six thematic working groups are:

-   [Fiscal Openness](http://www.opengovpartnership.org/groups/fiscal),
-   [Legislative
    Openness](http://www.opengovpartnership.org/groups/legislative),
-   [Anti-Corruption,](http://www.opengovpartnership.org/node/9232)
-   [Access to
    Information](http://www.opengovpartnership.org/groups/access-to-information),
-   [Open Data](http://www.opengovpartnership.org/groups/opendata/) and
-   [Openness in Natural
    Resources](http://www.opengovpartnership.org/node/5717)

To express your interest in joining a working group, visit the group’s
web page above, or
visit <http://www.opengovpartnership.org/who-we-are/ogp-working-groups>
